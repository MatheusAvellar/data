Pokémon Domains

This page contains 3 different datasets, all relating to Pokémon and internet domains.

everypokemon.csv (≈11 KB)
A list of every Pokémon name and their respective National Pokedéx numbers, as present in Bulbapedia.
pokemondomains.csv (≈13 KB)
A list of every Pokémon name that can become a domain (e.g. Pikachu -‍> pikac.hu). Includes the Pokémon's name, National Dex number, the TLD and the domain itself (e.g. "25, Pikachu, HU, pikac.hu"). As of the last update, this list contains 515 domains.
pokemondomains+status.csv (≈8 KB)
A list of the availability of each Pokémon domain; that is, if a program could detect that they have been purchased. STATUSES MAY NOT BE 100% CORRECT. Domains marked as available are not necessarily purchasable; the list only informs if it could figure out they have been purchased. As of the last update, there are 78 TAKEN domains and 437 AVAIL domains.

Last updated: 16 February 2022

This dataset is released as public domain. Attribution is appreciated but not required.
